# Writing Style Guide

## Audience

- Beginners
- Busy creators
- People who prefer plain language

## Rules

- Short sentences first
- Use concrete examples
- Explain why a step matters
- Avoid empty hype
- Prefer one useful workflow over ten vague tips

## Do not do

- Do not write like an ad
- Do not hide the real difficulty
- Do not use long abstract theory when a small example works better
